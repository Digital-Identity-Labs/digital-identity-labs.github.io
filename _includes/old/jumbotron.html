Digital Identity Ltd is a new Manchester-based company that supplies federated identity services to higher education. We are a small group of current and past employees of universities, motivated to make interesting and useful things for the UK academic community. Most of our work is based around applying the many benefits of Shibboleth and other SAML identity software to social web applications.

We plan to launch a series of small, experimental web applications in 2013 under the umbrella of our 'Digital Identity Labs' project. Popular services from the labs will be re-launched as full, supported subscription services.
